Services We Offer

Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

AFR provides legal and compliance advice and related services to a wide range of financial services and other businesses that are subject to an AML regulatory regime.

Banking And Securitisation

AFR provides a wide range of services to local banks and also acts in relation to cross-jurisdictional transactions with a Guernsey element. The firm also acts in relation to lending services and related documentation and undertakes enforcement work in respect of securities, schemes of arrangement, insolvencies and liquidations.

Company Formations

AFR advises and assists on all aspects of company formation.

Corporate Finance

AFR advises on all legal aspects in connection with the raising of capital in support of acquisitions, disposals, management buy-ins and buy-outs and mergers, together with all supporting due diligence.

Corporate Service Provider (CSP)

We offer a full range of fiduciary, management and administration services for offshore companies and trusts through our in-house service provider, AFR Trustees (Guernsey) Limited, which is fully licensed and regulated by the Guernsey Financial Services Commission.

Data Protection

The AFR team is able to provide advice and assistance in connection with data protection matters, both contentious and non-contentious.

Financial Services

AFR is able to offer comprehensive advice and assistance on all aspects of businesses in the financial services sector, including the drafting of policies and procedures manuals covering operations, processes, compliance, risk management and other regulatory issues.

Funds

AFR provides advice in respect of funds domiciled in Guernsey and other jurisdictions, in particular the establishment of investment funds, fund related transactions and on-going advice and assistance in relation to the structure of funds.

General Corporate

AFR advises and assists on corporate formations and restructuring together with all forms of corporate transactions, including mergers and acquisitions, shareholder arrangements and joint ventures.

Intellectual Property

AFR advises and assists on a wide range of matters related to intellectual property rights, including the ownership, registration, licensing and development of such rights and their enforcement.

Maritime

AFR can advise on all aspects of shipping law, including documentation, liabilities, collision, salvage, towage and wreck, pollution claims, the enforcement of maritime claims and insurance.

Utilities / Regulatory

AFR provides specialist legal advice, assistance and support for financial and professional services, telecommunications and other regulated sectors.
