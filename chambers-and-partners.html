Trust Formation & Advice

We are able to advise on the formation of personal, corporate and charitable trusts. We can also advise on existing trusts and settlements, including changes of trustees and distributions to beneficiaries. Through our trust company, AFR Trustees (Guernsey) Limited, we are able to provide services as a trustee.

Trust Disputes

We have advised on many types of disputes concerning trusts, including claims by beneficiaries, the removal of trustees, the oversight of trustees’ actions and the investment and management of trust assets.
