Profile

Martyn has worked in England as a Commercial lawyer for many years. After working in private practice for a boutique international law firm in London, he moved in-house and provided legal services for a number of different companies throughout the United Kingdom. In the last 10 years, he has been working for a variety of utility providers in the power sector, including on major infrastructure projects involving multiple international partners.
